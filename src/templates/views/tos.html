Trainers' Corner - Forum Guidelines

Welcome to Trainers' Corner, the online community dedicated to Pokémon video games! To ensure a positive and enjoyable experience for all members, we have established the following guidelines. By participating in this forum, you agree to adhere to these rules:

Respectful Communication:

Treat fellow trainers with kindness, respect, and courtesy. Avoid offensive language, personal attacks, or any form of discrimination.

Engage in constructive discussions and avoid excessive arguments or flame wars. Disagreements are allowed, but keep the conversation civil.

Relevant Content:

Focus discussions on Pokémon video games, including the main series, spin-offs, gameplay strategies, news, and updates.

Avoid unrelated topics or excessive promotion of external websites, products, or services.

Spoiler Policy:

When discussing new Pokémon games or storylines, use spoiler tags to prevent spoiling the experience for others who may not have played or completed the game yet.

Clearly label any posts containing spoilers and keep spoiler-related discussions confined to specific threads.

Intellectual Property and Legality:

Respect copyright laws and do not share or request illegal copies of Pokémon games, ROMs, or any unauthorized content.

Give credit to original creators when sharing fan art, fan fiction, or any other user-generated content.

Spam and Advertising:

Refrain from spamming the forum with repetitive or irrelevant posts. Keep discussions meaningful and on-topic.

Do not use the forum solely for advertising purposes, including promoting external products, services, or websites without prior permission.

Personal Information:

Protect your privacy and refrain from sharing personal contact information or any sensitive data on the forum. Be cautious while interacting with other members.

Moderation and Enforcement:

Follow the instructions of forum moderators. They are here to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone.

Report any posts or users violating the guidelines to the moderators for appropriate action.

Remember, Trainers' Corner is a place to connect with fellow Pokémon enthusiasts and share your love for the games. Let's foster a welcoming and inclusive community where trainers can learn, discuss, and have fun together!

By using this forum, you acknowledge and agree to abide by these guidelines. Enjoy your time at Trainers' Corner, and happy gaming!

Evzs & RathGate, Trainers' Corner team
